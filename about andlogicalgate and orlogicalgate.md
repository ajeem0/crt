# about andlogicalgate and orlogicalgate

Agar AND logical gate mein agar condition false Ho, Jaye, to uske aage ki condition ko check Nahi Karte matlab, Aage Ki condition Mein Koii expression, Hoto vo execute nahi Hoga

Agar OR logical gate mein agar condition true Ho, Jaye, to uske aage ki condition ko check Nahi Karte matlab, Aage Ki condition Mein Koii expression, Hoto vo execute nahi Hoga
